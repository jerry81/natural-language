Die Katze (the cat) -> Die Katzen (the cats)

German nouns have grammatical gender: masculine, feminine, and neuter.

Masculine: der (the)
Feminine: die (the)
Neuter: das (the)
The indefinite articles are:
Masculine: ein (a/an)
